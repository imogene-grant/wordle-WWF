How To Play

Welcome to WWF Conservation Word of the Day

  • Each day, there’s a new conservation-themed 5-letter word to guess.
  • You have 6 chances to figure it out.
  • As you guess, the tiles will give you hints:
  • A gray tile means the letter isn’t in the word at all.
  • A yellow tile means the letter is in the word, but not in the right spot.
  • A green tile means the letter is in the correct spot.
  • Every word is related to wildlife, climate, or the natural world—just like WWF’s mission.
  • You can only use real 5-letter words—no shortcuts!
  • And yes, the word changes every day. Come back tomorrow for a new one 🌱

Tip: After clicking “Generate New Word,” tap anywhere else on the page to continue typing.

Good luck, and thank you for supporting conservation in a fun way!
